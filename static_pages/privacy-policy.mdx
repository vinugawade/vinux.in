At [vinux.in](http://vinux.in), we value your privacy and take it seriously. We do not collect any personal information from users, including but not limited to names, email addresses, and other contact information.

We do keep track of visitor count for certain pages (blogs) on the website, but this information is not linked to any specific user and is used for internal analytics purposes only.

We currently do not have a newsletter feature enabled. However, if a newsletter feature is added in the future, your email address would be handled with care. We will only use it for the purpose of sending future newsletters that we publish.

We don't share any information collected through this website with any third parties, except as required by law.

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time, and will notify you of any changes by posting the updated policy.

If you have any questions or concerns about the Privacy Policy, please contact us at [vinulike11@gmail.com](mailto:vinulike11@gmail.com).
